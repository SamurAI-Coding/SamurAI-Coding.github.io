SamurAI 3x3 3016 Game Rule Summary (3/3)

  • Enemy samurai in occupied cells are injured.
    They are moved back to their home positions, and cannot move for next 18 turns.
  • Vision is limited to cells around friend army.
    Click the image below to see views of each army.
  • Each samurai can take action once in one period of six turns.
  • One game consists of eight periods = 96 turns.
  • A match consists of two games with alternate first player.
  • The wide with larger total territories of two games wins the match.
