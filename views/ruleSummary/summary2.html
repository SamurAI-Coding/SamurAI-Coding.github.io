SamurAI 3x3 3016 Game Rule Summary (2/3)

  • In one turn, one of the samurai makes a sequence of actions:
    • Moving to one of four adjacent cells ($2),
    • Occupying cells nearby in one direction ($4), or
    • Hiding or disclosing oneself ($1).
    within the budget of $7.
  • Cells occupied depend on the weapon.
