IPSJ International AI Programming Contest: SamurAI Coding 2015-16

As the Internet industry rapidly grows, there is an intensifying need for high quality engineers and programmers. Information Processing Society of Japan (IPSJ) conducted the International AI programming Contest "SamurAI Coding" in 2012, 2013 and 2014, aiming to cultivate the younger generation so they can become leading researchers, developers and successful on global level (1st SamurAI Coding Final Photos in 2012, 2nd Final Results in 2013 and Results in 2014-15).

4th SamurAI Coding will be held between October 2015 and March 2016. Contestants will compete their engineering skills for AI game programming. Winners of the preliminary round will move on to the final round, which will be held on March 11 2016 collocated with 78th IPSJ National Convention at Keio University, Yagami Campus. Participating engineers and programmers will be able to demonstrate their skills on a global level through this contest.
