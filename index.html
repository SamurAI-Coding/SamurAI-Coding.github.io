IPSJ International AI Programming Contest: SamurAI Coding 2014-15

As the Internet industry rapidly grows, there is an intensifying need for high quality engineers and programmers. Information Processing Society of Japan (IPSJ) conducted the international AI programming contest "SamurAI Coding" in 2012 and 2013, aimings to cultivate the younger generation so they can become leading researchers, developers and successful on global level (1st SamurAI Coding Final Photos in 2012 and 2nd Final Results in 2013).

3rd SamurAI Coding will held between September 2014 and March 2015. Contestants will be competing their engineering skills for AI game programming and the winners of the preliminary round will move on to the final round, which will be held on March 18 2015 collocated with 77th IPSJ National Convention in Kyoto (TBD). Participating engineers will be able to measure their skills on a global level through this contest.

News

  • Nov 7: The online execution server has been opened. Note that the game rule is still beta. We plan to update the game rule in few days.
  • May 1: This preliminary website has been launched.

Prize

World Final contestants who are not IPSJ members will receive one-year IPSJ membership without any entry fee and annual fee. (The entry fee is originally necessary for IPSJ regular members.)

Travel expenses of World Final contestants will be partially supported.

All World Final contestants will be presented on the contest site.

Important Dates

  • Rule and platform announcement, submission open: November 14th, 2014
  • Preliminary round submission due: December 21st , 2014
  • Preliminary round result announcement: December 22nd, 2014
  • World final round in Kyoto (collocated with 77th IPSJ Annual Convention): March 18, 2015
