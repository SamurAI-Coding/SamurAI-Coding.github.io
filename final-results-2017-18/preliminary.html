Preliminary Round Tournament
  • A round-robin tournament of all 36 participating teams,
    i.e., all the teams play against all the other teams.
  • A total of 630 matches (1,260 races) were played in the tournament.
  • The 35 matches with different opponents are on different race courses.
  • For each match, points given to the teams are:
    • 2 points to the match winner,
    • 1 point each for a draw match, and
    • 0 point to the loser.
    i.e., winning all 35 matches would result in 70 points.
  • Ranks are decided on the following criteria, in this order.
    • Higher total points
    • Lower total time
