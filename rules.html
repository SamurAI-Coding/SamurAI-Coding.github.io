SamurAI Coding Rules

SamurAI Coding is a programming contest in which programmers compete for their superiority in AI programming skills. In the contest, the game named SamurAI 3x3 (samurai three on three) is played by two armies of three samurai each, and each of participating AI programs controls one of the samurai in each game. Many games with different army composition are played in a contest and the winner will be the program controlling the samurai with the highest total score.
